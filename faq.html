Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bible Kids free?

Yes! All stories, quizzes, and games are free for families.

Is the content safe for children?

Absolutely — all content is age-appropriate, positive, and Bible-based.

Do I need an account?

Parents create an account so they can manage content and monitor progress.

Can I add multiple children?

Yes — you can add as many children as you'd like.

How do quizzes work?

Each story includes a short quiz that helps reinforce learning.

Which devices are supported?

Phones, tablets, and desktop computers — all fully responsive.
